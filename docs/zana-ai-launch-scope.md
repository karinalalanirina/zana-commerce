# Zana AI Launch Scope

No new AI features were built in this pass. This file scopes what is safe to use from the existing WADesk base.

| AI Use Case | Launch decision | Human review needed? | Why | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Campaign/template copy drafting | Safe to launch now | Yes | Low operational risk if reviewed before send | Good first AI use. |
| Operator reply suggestions | Safe to launch now | Yes | Human stays in the loop | Best fit for managed pilots. |
| Supervised FAQ assistance | Launch with human review | Yes | Useful, but should not send unattended | Keep within operator workflow. |
| Product Q&A drafting | Launch with human review | Yes | Risk of wrong stock/price claims | Use as draft only. |
| Payment reminder drafting | Launch with human review | Yes | Payment wording should stay operator-controlled | Do not auto-send. |
| Abandoned inquiry follow-up drafting | Launch with human review | Yes | Can sound spammy or mistimed without review | Good later workflow aid. |
| Repeat purchase drafting | Launch with human review | Yes | Good retention use case, but still needs consent discipline | Review before send. |
| Delivery answers | Needs guardrails before automation | Yes | Can misstate fees, routes, timing | Do not automate yet. |
| Payment instructions | Needs guardrails before automation | Yes | Wrong instructions create direct revenue risk | Keep manual. |
| Order-intent automation | Needs guardrails before automation | Yes | Risk of false order creation | Defer. |
| Automated reminders | Needs guardrails before automation | Yes | Consent and timing risk | Defer. |
| Unsupervised payment automation | Do not use yet | N/A | High operational and trust risk | Not launch-safe. |
| Unsupervised delivery automation | Do not use yet | N/A | High operational risk | Not launch-safe. |
| Unsupported multilingual claims | Do not use yet | N/A | Swahili/Sheng quality not yet validated for launch claims | Keep marketing conservative. |
